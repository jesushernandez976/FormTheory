About Form Theory

Seeing a chiropractor can improve mobility, reduce pain, and help you move with confidence, whether you’re recovering from an injury or just dealing with the daily aches that slow you down. At Form Theory Chiropractic, you get care that’s tailored to how your body works. Form Theory is here to help you manage pain, restore proper movement, and get back to performing at your best.

When people come to Form Theory for the first time, what stands out is the level of attention and clarity they receive. In your initial visit, we take the time to listen to your goals, assess how your joints and muscles are moving, and identify the real source of your pain or limitations.

From there, we create a clear, individualized plan, simple steps, measurable progress, and care that actually makes sense. Depending on what your body needs, your sessions may include a blend of chiropractic adjustments, soft-tissue work, and targeted movement or stability drills to get results quickly and efficiently.

We believe care works best when you understand what’s happening in your body. That’s why we also provide education, self-care strategies, and at-home progressions to help you stay on track between visits. The goal is simple:
reduce pain, improve movement, and give you the confidence to live and train without limits.

Dr. Logan Donaldson

Founder, Form Theory

I founded Form Theory to help people move without limits, whether that’s running faster, lifting your kids without pain, or staying active for life.
My background spans form growing up in the Midwest, serving in the U.S. Air Force, personal training, and pushing my own limits through CrossFit, marathons, and obstacle races.
Those experiences taught me that strength and resilience aren’t just for athletes, they’re skills anyone can build.
